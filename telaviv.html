The City

Tel Aviv–Yafo

Tel Aviv is one of the most vibrant and welcoming cities in the Middle East — a perfect destination for players, coaches and families visiting for the tournament.

Weather in May
Warm & Sunny
Temperatures range from 18°C at night to 28°C during the day. Very low chance of rain. Perfect conditions for sport and sightseeing.
Currency
Israeli Shekel (₪)
Tournament packages are priced in EUR. Major credit cards widely accepted. ATMs at the airport, Azrieli Center, and throughout the city.
Language
Hebrew & English
Hebrew is the official language. English is very widely spoken in Tel Aviv, especially in hotels, restaurants and tourist areas.
Things to Do

Explore Tel Aviv

Make the most of your time in Tel Aviv. The city has plenty to offer for players and accompanying families between matches.

Old Jaffa (Yafo)
Ancient Port City
Just 4 km from the venue, the ancient port of Jaffa features stunning views, galleries, restaurants and one of the oldest flea markets in the world.
The Beach
Tel Aviv Coastline
Tel Aviv boasts 14 km of Mediterranean beaches. Gordon Beach and Frishman Beach are popular, clean, and just a short taxi or light rail ride away.
Carmel Market
Shuk HaCarmel
Tel Aviv's famous open-air market is a colourful sensory experience — street food, fresh produce, clothing and souvenirs, 10 min from the city centre.
Azrieli Center
Mall & Observation
The Azrieli towers are directly next to the official hotel. The complex includes a large shopping mall, restaurants, cinema and a rooftop observation deck.
Neve Tzedek
Historic Quarter
One of Tel Aviv's oldest and most charming neighbourhoods, filled with boutique shops, art galleries, cafés and beautiful restored architecture.
Jerusalem
Day Trip
Jerusalem is just 45 minutes by train from Tel Aviv. For families with extra time, a visit to one of the world's most historic cities is unmissable.
