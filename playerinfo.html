Player Information & FAQ

Equipment · Rules · Logistics
Essential Info

Quick Reference

Official Ball
Double Yellow Dot
WSF approved double yellow dot ball used in all age categories U15 and above.
U13 & U11: single yellow dot
Dress Code
Squash Attire
Clean squash attire required. No football/basketball clothing. White soles or non-marking shoes mandatory.
Eye Protection
Recommended
Eye protection is mandatory for all competing players in all age categories, in line with ESF Junior Circuit requirements.
Warm-up Time
5 Minutes
Each match includes a 5-minute on-court warm-up before play begins. Players must be at the court 10 minutes before their match.
On the Day

What to Expect

Court Access
Courts open at 07:30 for warm-up. All matches run 08:00–18:00 on competition days.
Check-in
Players must check in at the tournament office on arrival. The office is located 10 metres from the courts.
Meals
Lunch and dinner served at the venue for players on the meal package. On-site club restaurant also available.
Medical
On-site first aider present during all match hours. Physio available on request (additional charge). Nearest hospital ~8 min by car.
Wi-Fi & Connectivity
Free Wi-Fi available throughout the club. A WhatsApp group will be created for all participants before the event.
Squash Shop
On-site squash shop with racket stringing service available throughout the event.
Rules & Conduct

Player Conduct

ESF Code of Conduct
All players and coaches are expected to abide by the ESF Junior Circuit Code of Conduct throughout the event.
Sportsmanship
Respect for opponents, officials and spectators is required at all times. Poor conduct may result in conduct warnings or disqualification.
Match Punctuality
Players must report to their assigned court at least 10 minutes before their scheduled match time. Late arrival may result in a forfeit.
Coaching During Matches
Coaching is permitted between games only. No coaching or instruction is allowed during rallies or between points.
Scoring System
PAR 11 point-a-rally scoring. Best of 5 games. ESF standard match format applies to all categories.
