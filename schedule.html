How It Works

Schedule Information

Publication
~4 Days Before
First match times for all players will be posted here approximately 4 days before the tournament starts.
Daily Schedule
Courts 08:00 · Play 10:00
Courts are available from 08:00 each tournament day, and play begins at 10:00. On the final day, Sunday 16 May, the finals begin at 14:00, followed immediately by the prize ceremony. Each player's exact first-match time is published about 4 days before the event.
4 Courts
Court 1 – 4
All 4 courts will be in use throughout the tournament. Courts 1 and 2 have spectator seating.
