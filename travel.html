Step by Step

Getting Here

Travelling to the Israel Junior Open 2027 is straightforward. The airport, hotel and venue are all excellently connected.

1
Fly into Ben Gurion International Airport (TLV)
Tel Aviv is served by Ben Gurion International Airport (IATA: TLV), located approximately 20 km south-east of the city centre. Direct flights are available from most major European cities and many international hubs. The airport is modern and well-connected.
2
Take the direct train to Tel Aviv HaShalom Station
From the airport, board the direct train to Tel Aviv HaShalom Station. The journey takes approximately 20–25 minutes and trains run frequently throughout the day. No connection required — it is a direct service. The train departs from Terminal 3 at Ben Gurion Airport.
3
2-minute walk to the Crowne Plaza (official hotel)
Tel Aviv HaShalom Station is located directly beneath the Azrieli Center complex. The Crowne Plaza Tel Aviv City Center is a 2-minute walk from the station exit, within the same complex. No taxi or shuttle required from the station to the hotel.
4
5-minute walk from hotel to courts
The Migdali Tel Aviv Country Club is located 0.7 km (approximately 5 minutes walk) from the Crowne Plaza. There is no shuttle between the hotel and the venue. Light rail and taxis are also available from the hotel if preferred.
Arrival Support
All participants will receive detailed arrival instructions by email prior to the event. A dedicated WhatsApp group will be created for all attendees. Players who book the airport shuttle service will be met in the Arrivals Hall at Ben Gurion Airport by an Israel Squash Association representative. Full details will be included in the official tournament invitation.

About Tel Aviv

Welcome to Tel Aviv

Tel Aviv is one of the Middle East's most vibrant cities — a perfect destination for players and families visiting for the tournament.

Weather in May
Warm & Sunny
Expect temperatures of 22–28°C. Low rainfall. Perfect conditions for a sporting trip.
On-site
Shops & Restaurants
The Azrieli Center and Migdali complex both offer restaurants, cafés, supermarkets and shops within walking distance.
Local Transport
Light Rail & Taxis
Tel Aviv's light rail, buses and taxis provide easy access to the beach, the Old City of Jaffa, and all major attractions.

The Israel Junior Open 2027 is an official ESF Junior Circuit Bronze event, organised by the Israel Squash Association.
