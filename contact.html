Before You Register

Key Information

Deadlines & Requirements

  • Entry deadline: 1 May 2027 — late entries subject to availability
  • Entry fee: €65 per player — mandatory, includes ESF ranking fee (€6) + handouts
  • Hotel packages are optional; entry fee is the only mandatory payment
  • Meal package (€75) can be added independently of hotel booking
  • Triple rooms are not available for this event
  • Airport transfer included in all hotel packages (by arrangement)
  • Official invitation with final pricing will be sent by ESF
  • Arrival instructions & WhatsApp group details sent before the event
How to Enter
Entry via ESF System
Entries are submitted through the official ESF Junior Circuit entry system. Please contact us directly if you need assistance with the submission process or have questions about packages and payments.
